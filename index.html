Marathwada Environment Care Cluster was formed to protect our mother earth from pollution. Organizations such as MASSIA, AISA, WIA, CII, CMIA, Vyapari Mahasangh, MGM, MIT, MCTC, MIDC and MPCB have joined hands to form this cluster. MECC is working on realizing the dream of clean & green Waluj MIDC on a highly sustainable basis. Under the MECC banner every year over 8000 people from industry gather to take the Clean India pledge. We are extending MECC areas of operation to Chattrapati Sambhajinagar city in this phase.

While extending the work of the MECC in theCity of Chattrapati Sambhajinagar it was launched under the Project Chattrapati Sambhajinagar First. In 2018 Company got Chattrapati Sambhajinagar First registered as an official Trademark under social platform. This initiative is dedicated for the betterment of the City of Chattrapati Sambhajinagar. “City before Self”
